About Fuel Mate

Fuel Mate is more than just a dashboard, it's a solution born from a real need for smarter, more transparent access to petrol station services in Malaysia. Designed with users in mind, FuelMate provides meaningful insights on reviews, ratings, services, and facilities at petrol stations across the country.

How It All Started

The idea for Fuel Mate came during my final year of studying Information Technology at UiTM Perlis. As someone who frequently drives long distances, I often struggled to find accurate, up-to-date information about petrol stations, especially regarding facilities like toilets, suraus, convenience stores, or EV chargers.

Combining my passion for clean user interfaces and data-driven solutions, I began building a dashboard that could help others like me. After months of data collection, API integration, design, and development, FuelMate was born.
